About

Display: 6.78-inch AMOLED, 1080x2436 pixels, 144Hz refresh rate, 1300 nits peak brightness, Always-On Display. Chipset: MediaTek Helio G100 Ultimate (6nm), Octa-core CPU (2x2.2 GHz Cortex-A76 & 6x2.0 GHz Cortex-A55), Mali-G57 MC2 GPU. RAM/Storage: 8GB or 12GB RAM, 256GB UFS 2.2 storage. Camera: Rear: 50MP main (Samsung GN5, f/1.9, OIS), 8MP ultrawide (112° FOV), flicker sensor. Front: 32MP selfie camera (f/2.2). Battery: 5200mAh with 90W wired charging (100% in 38 minutes), 30W wireless MagCharge, 10W reverse wired charging, reverse wireless charging, bypass charging. Audio: Stereo speakers tuned by JBL, 24-bit/192kHz Hi-Res & Hi-Res wireless audio, no 3.5mm headphone jack. Connectivity: 4G LTE, Wi-Fi 5 (802.11 a/b/g/n/ac), Bluetooth 5.4, USB Type-C 2.0 with OTG support, NFC (market/region dependent), infrared port, FM radio. Build: Glass front, aerospace-grade aluminum frame, IP64 rating (dust tight and water-resistant), RGB notification light on the back. Design: Sleek design with Bio-Active Halo AI Lighting for notifications and health monitoring, available in Titanium Grey, Enchanted Purple, Racing Edition, and Shadow Black.
