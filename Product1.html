About

Display: 6.78-inch AMOLED, 1080x2436 pixels, 144Hz refresh rate, 1300 nits peak brightness, 2160Hz PWM dimming, TÜV Low Blue Light certified, Always-On Display. Chipset: MediaTek Dimensity 8350 Ultimate (4nm), Octa-core CPU (1x3.35 GHz Cortex-A715 & 3x3.20 GHz Cortex-A715 & 4x2.20 GHz Cortex-A510), Mali-G615 MC6 GPU. RAM/Storage: 12GB LPDDR5X RAM, 256GB UFS 4.0 storage, no microSD card slot. Camera: Rear: 50MP Sony IMX896 main sensor (f/1.9, OIS), 50MP periscope telephoto (3x optical zoom, 100x digital zoom, OIS), 8MP ultrawide (112° FOV). Front: 32MP selfie camera (f/2.2). Battery: 5200mAh with 100W wired charging (100% in 32 minutes), 50W wireless MagCharge, 10W reverse wired charging, 7.5W reverse wireless charging, bypass charging. Audio: Stereo speakers tuned by JBL, 24-bit/192kHz Hi-Res & Hi-Res wireless audio, no 3.5mm headphone jack. Connectivity: 5G SA/NSA/Sub6, 4G LTE, Wi-Fi 6 (802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/ax), Bluetooth 5.4, USB Type-C 2.0 with OTG support, GPS, NFC, infrared port, FM radio. Build: Glass front, aerospace-grade aluminum frame, IP64 rating (dust tight and water-resistant), RGB notification light on the back. Design: Sleek design with Bio-Active Halo AI Lighting for notifications and health monitoring, available in Titanium Grey, Enchanted Purple, and Racing Edition.
